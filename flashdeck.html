About

WHAT IS FLASHDECK?

Flashdeck takes the friction out of studying. Paste any text — notes, articles, passages — choose how you want it chopped, and Flashdeck generates a clean, ready-to-study deck of flashcards in seconds. No account. No setup. Just learning.

Features

WHAT IT DOES

Smart Chop Paste any text and let Flashdeck segment it into cards — short, medium, or large chop sizes.
Tap to Peek Reveal answers your way — tap to peek or tap to flip, your choice.
Offline Ready No internet required once your deck is generated. Study anywhere.
No Account Required Open it and go. Nothing to sign up for.
